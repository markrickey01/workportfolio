Committee Service Application

We’re seeking steady, thoughtful people who can help steer projects, events, and investment in Historic Downtown Kendallville. Committees meet roughly once per month and support real work between meetings.

How Committees Work

Each committee focuses on a core part of the Main Street mission. Members:

  • Attend regular meetings (typically 1–2 hours per month)
  • Help evaluate ideas and set priorities
  • Take on small action items between meetings
  • Serve as ambassadors for downtown in their own circles
