Volunteer with Historic Downtown Kendallville

Not everyone can write a big check — but a few solid hours of help at the right time can make an event or project actually happen. If you’re willing to show up and pitch in, we want you on the list.

Ways to Help

We’re building a simple roster of people who can be called on for:

  • Event setup and teardown (tents, tables, signage)
  • Check-in, information tables, and wayfinding
  • Photography, social media support, and content capture
  • Beautification days: planting, cleanup, light maintenance
  • Special projects at 231/234 Main and along the corridor
All ages & skills welcome
